Our Passion

Where tradition meets excellence in Oak Forest

From our 800° woodfired oven to your table, every pizza is a masterpiece of Italian tradition.

Bollo Oak Forest serves authentic Neapolitan pizza in the heart of Garden Oaks. Our passion for perfection has earned us recognition from Gayot.com as one of the Top 10 Pizza Restaurants in the Country in 2022, and we've been honored as Houston's best pizza by PaperCity & OutSmart magazine.

Our menu features traditional and creative pizzas, fresh salads, and delectable desserts. Each pizza is hand-stretched and baked to perfection in our woodfired oven, creating that signature charred crust and melted cheese that defines true Neapolitan pizza.
